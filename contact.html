Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel or modify my booking?

You can cancel or modify your booking up to 2 hours before the showtime. Contact our support team or use your booking confirmation email.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Can I choose my seats?

Absolutely! Our interactive seating chart lets you choose your preferred seats in real-time. You can see which seats are available, occupied, or already selected.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! We offer special rates for groups of 10 or more. Contact our sales team for custom pricing and arrangements.

What if I arrive late to the movie?

We recommend arriving 15 minutes before showtime. Late arrivals may not be admitted during certain scenes to avoid disrupting other guests.
